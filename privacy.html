Privacy Policy

Who we are

Pip Smart Tags ("we", "us") makes NFC pet ID tags. We're based in Northern Ireland, UK. Contact: pipknowstheway@gmail.com.

What we collect and why

When you register a tag you choose what to share. Depending on what you enter, we store: your pet's details and photo, your name, phone number and email, optional home address, optional vet/medical/microchip/insurance details, and optional emergency contacts. We use this for exactly one purpose: showing it to someone who scans your pet's tag so they can reunite you, plus optional service emails you switch on (health reminders, scan alerts).

What a scanner sees

Anyone who taps your pet's tag sees the profile you've chosen to publish. You control visibility of sensitive fields (address, medical info, microchip) from your edit page, and can change or remove anything at any time. Your home address is only shown when you choose to show it (for example in Lost Mode).

Finder location

If someone who finds your pet chooses to share their location, that location is sent to you, the owner. We never track anyone's location in the background — sharing is a deliberate, one-tap choice by the finder.

Where data is stored

Your data is stored securely in Google's cloud services and shown via our website hosted on Netlify. Both providers process data under their own security and GDPR commitments. Tag profile links use randomised codes that cannot practicably be guessed.

What we never do

We don't sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for anything other than running the tag service.

How long we keep it

For as long as your tag is active. If you stop using the service, email us and we'll delete your profile.

Your rights (UK GDPR)

You can ask us at any time to see the data we hold about you, correct it, or delete it entirely. Email pipknowstheway@gmail.com and we'll act within one month, usually much faster. If you're unhappy with how we handle your data you can complain to the ICO (ico.org.uk).

Cookies

The tag pages don't use advertising or tracking cookies.

Children

Tags should be registered by an adult; we don't knowingly collect children's data.

Changes

If this policy changes materially we'll note the date here and, where possible, email registered owners.

Terms of Sale

  1. The product. A Pip Smart Tag is a physical NFC pet tag plus access to our online pet profile service. The service requires the finder to have an NFC-capable smartphone or to scan the backup QR code, plus an internet connection.
  2. What we promise. We'll keep the tag service running with reasonable care. The service relies on third-party infrastructure (website hosting and cloud data storage); brief outages beyond our control can occur. A tag is an aid to reuniting you with your pet — it is not a guarantee of recovery, and it is not a substitute for a microchip or a legally required collar ID tag (in the UK, dogs in public must wear a tag showing the owner's name and address — check your own compliance).
  3. Activation. Each tag comes with an activation code. Keep it safe until registered; anyone with the code and the tag can claim it. Once claimed, the tag locks to the owner.
  4. Returns (UK consumer law). You may cancel an online order within 14 days of delivery for a full refund, and return faulty goods within 30 days. Tags that fail to scan due to a manufacturing fault will be replaced free. Normal wear, dog damage, or loss of the tag isn't covered — contact us for replacement pricing.
  5. Your responsibilities. Keep your profile details accurate, and don't put anything unlawful or offensive in a public profile — we can suspend profiles that abuse the service.
  6. Service changes. If we ever discontinue the service we'll give registered owners at least 60 days' notice by email.
  7. Liability. Nothing in these terms limits liability that cannot be limited by law. Otherwise, our total liability is limited to the price you paid for the tag.
